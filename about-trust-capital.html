About Us

Turning Scrap Into Sustainable Value

Trusted Capital LLC is a leading Emirati company specializing in the processing and trading of ferrous and non-ferrous metal scrap. With operations across several countries, we are recognized for our strong global presence, reliable trading practices, and consistent commitment to quality.

We buy, recycle, and supply a wide range of metals — including aluminum, copper, brass, lead, stainless steel, and nickel alloys — serving industries around the world. Our advanced facilities, skilled workforce, and strict adherence to international standards make us one of the most dependable names in the metal recycling sector.

Our Journey

Built on Trust. Driven by Quality.

Founded in the UAE, Trusted Capital began with a simple belief — that every piece of scrap metal carries potential value for both industry and the environment. Over the years, we have transformed this belief into a thriving global business built on transparency, efficiency, and sustainability.

With a growing network of yards, production sites, and export operations, we ensure every transaction is handled with precision and care. Our customers rely on us for competitive pricing, quick turnaround, and traceable logistics that simplify the scrap trading process worldwide.

Our Journey

Sustainability at the Core of Our Business

We take pride in being a responsible recycler that contributes to a cleaner, greener future. Through sustainable sourcing and recycling practices, we minimize waste, reduce emissions, and conserve natural resources.
