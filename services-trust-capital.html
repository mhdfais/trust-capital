Our offerings & Services

Reliable Recycling. Resonsible Trading. Global Reach.

At Trusted Capital, We deliver full spectrum metal-scrap solutions tailored for modern industries. Driven by quality, transparency, and sustainablity, our services turn metal waste into valuable resources--helping clients achieve their goals while supporting a circular economy.

01

Metal Scrap Recycling

We specialize in the recycling and processing of ferrous and non-ferrous metals, including aluminum, copper, brass, stainless steel, and more.
Our facilities use modern equipment and environmentally safe methods to recover maximum metal value while minimizing waste.
By turning discarded materials into reusable resources, we contribute to a circular economy that benefits both business and the planet.

02

Metal Trading

With a strong global network, Trusted Capital connects suppliers and buyers across multiple markets.
We offer competitive pricing, transparent transactions, and timely deliveries of high-quality recycled metals.
Every trade is backed by our commitment to integrity, ensuring long-term partnerships and consistent value for our clients.

03

Collection & Sorting

Our collection and sorting services ensure that every type of scrap metal is efficiently handled and categorized based on composition and quality.
Using advanced sorting techniques, we maintain the highest standards of safety and precision.
Whether it’s industrial scrap or bulk consignments, our team ensures smooth operations from pickup to processing.

04

Export & Logistics

We provide complete export and logistics support for international clients, ensuring fast, compliant, and reliable shipments.
From container loading and documentation to customs clearance and delivery, our logistics experts handle every detail with precision.
With global operations and ISO-certified facilities, Trusted Capital guarantees efficient supply chain management from start to finish.
